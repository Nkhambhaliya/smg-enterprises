Our Products

Comprehensive range of high-quality shuttering, scaffolding, and formwork systems manufactured in-house.

Scaffolding

Our scaffolding systems are designed for safety and efficiency, providing a secure working platform at any height.

Cuplock Verticals

The foundation of our cuplock system, these verticals provide strong and stable support. The cup and blade system allows for quick and easy erection and dismantling.

Horizontals / Ledgers

Ledgers lock into the cuplock verticals to create a rigid and secure scaffolding structure. Available in multiple lengths.
